Display kitchen/order status for diners
When enabled, diners can see the kitchen status of each item (e.g. sending, preparing, delivered) in their Check Orders view.
Require customers to pay on phone
When enabled, customers must complete payment on their phone before the order is sent to the kitchen. Requires online payment (Payment Asia) to be configured. When disabled, orders are confirmed and sent immediately — staff collect payment at the table or counter.
Show order preparation status
When enabled, customers see their order number and preparation status after payment. Default: on for takeaway, off for table service.
